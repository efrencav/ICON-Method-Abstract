Patco Manufacturing: Digital Evolution & Sales Enablement

Client

Patco Manufacturing

The Challenge

Patco had strong capabilities but a dated brand experience and an RFQ flow that didn’t guide buyers. Content varied by author, visuals drifted, and lead handoffs lacked structure.

Symptoms we found

  • Commodity messaging—features over outcomes and proof.
  • Inconsistent visuals across site and sales PDFs.
  • Slow page production; little component reuse.
  • CRM fields/stages misaligned to actual handoffs.

Approach

We ran a focused ICON sprint: sharpened positioning and narrative, installed a componentized CMS, and rebuilt the RFQ flow with clear proof and calls to action. Analytics and CRM hygiene locked feedback loops.

What we implemented

  • Identity: messaging map, expert voice, and refreshed visual kit.
  • Communication: conversion-first copy for hero, categories, and RFQ.
  • Optimization: CMS component system + page templates; event tracking.
  • Navigation: KPI dashboard, weekly reviews, and SLA-based handoffs.

Solution

A modern brand system and a faster content engine. We standardized voice/proof, built reusable page modules, and created sales-ready PDFs to support quoting conversations.

  • Positioning & Narrative System: claims, objections, proof matrix.
  • Reusable Components: hero, categories, proof, RFQ, CTA, resource tiles.
  • Sales Enablement: capability one-pagers and quote-support sheets.
