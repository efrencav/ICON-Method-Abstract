Branding and Packaging

Cafe Diario

Brand development, packaging, and a marketing strategy for Cafe Diario — a daily coffee ritual brought to life with a premium identity, shelf-ready packaging, and a launch plan that wins retail and online.

The Challenge

Create a distinctive coffee brand from the ground up — identity, packaging, and marketing — that stands out on crowded shelves and carries through to ecommerce without losing the craft story.

What We Did

Built the Cafe Diario brand platform from scratch — naming story, visual identity, and packaging system — to balance craft credibility with retail impact. Designed bold labels and secondary packaging for flavor differentiation and shelf blocking, and developed a marketing strategy that connects the brand story across retail placements and digital campaigns.
