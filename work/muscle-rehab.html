Muscle Rehab

Muscle Rehab is an athletic recovery bath bomb built for people who train hard and want relief fast. In a crowded category, the benefit and credibility must be obvious immediately.

The engagement locked a clear product position and message, then translated it into identity and packaging designed to be understood in seconds and to convert without hype.

Athletes decide quickly. If the benefit is unclear or the product looks untrustworthy, they move on. The brand had to communicate “fast relief, built for training” without forcing interpretation.

We simplified the story, sharpened the promise, and built packaging and messaging that signal efficacy, safety, and a repeatable recovery ritual.

Since launch in 2018, the product has generated $700k+ in profits, supported by clear positioning, disciplined messaging, and packaging that converts.

Strategic Intervention

The core decision was to prioritize instant comprehension and athletic credibility. Positioning and messaging were defined to communicate a single promise: fast relief for people who train hard, without exaggerated claims or vague wellness language.

Positioning, message hierarchy, and identity were locked as one system so every visual and verbal element reinforced trust, efficacy cues, and repeat purchase confidence. Execution followed only after the standard was fixed.

Why It Matters

Performance categories reward clarity. When the buyer is skeptical and moving fast, credibility and comprehension determine conversion.

This work shows what happens when positioning is locked before execution: hesitation drops, trust increases, and the product can scale without constantly changing the story.
