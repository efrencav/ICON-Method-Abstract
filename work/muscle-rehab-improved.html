Muscle Rehab: Athletic Recovery Brand

$700K+ Profit Since Launch

Strategic positioning and conversion-optimized packaging designed for 3-second shelf comprehension. When positioning drives execution, brands convert faster and scale consistently.

The Challenge

Athletes decide in seconds. In a crowded recovery market flooded with vague wellness claims, Muscle Rehab faced a critical challenge: communicate real performance benefits with absolute clarity at first glance. Any confusion, softness, or lack of credibility meant instant rejection. The brand needed to cut through noise, establish trust immediately, and make its purpose unmistakable—before the decision window closed.

Strategy

Position before execution. We locked a singular promise—fast relief for people who train hard—then built every visual and verbal element to communicate it in under 3 seconds.

1. Instant Comprehension

Benefit clear at shelf distance, no interpretation needed.

2. Athletic Credibility

Visual and verbal cues that signal efficacy and trust, not lifestyle fluff.

3. Conversion Architecture

Packaging and messaging designed to eliminate hesitation and drive repeat purchase.

Deliverables

  • Brand positioning & messaging architecture
  • Complete visual identity system
  • Packaging design (single & multi-pack retail/ecommerce)
  • Brand guidelines & go-to-market assets
