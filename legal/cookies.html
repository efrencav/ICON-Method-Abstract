Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your device and remember information about your visit, like your preferred language and other settings. Cookies make browsing more efficient and improve your overall experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • Improve Website Functionality: Ensure the site loads properly and functions as intended.
  • Analyze Site Traffic: Understand how visitors interact with our website to optimize content and structure.
  • Enhance User Experience: Remember your preferences for faster, more personalized browsing.
  • Support Marketing Activities: Deliver relevant advertising and track campaign performance, when applicable.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic site functions. Without these, the site may not perform properly.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous information about how visitors use the site. Used for improving performance and user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember user choices (such as language or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track user activity across websites to deliver targeted advertising and help measure the effectiveness of marketing efforts.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may impact your experience on our site.

  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
