The Kinsmen Group: Transformation for Growth

Client

The Kinsmen Group

The Challenge

High-value buyers, complex offerings, and long cycles. Early materials were feature-heavy, visually inconsistent, and changed voice by author—slowing trust and confusing value.

Symptoms we found

  • Inconsistent story across site, sales, and proposals.
  • Feature-first copy, unclear outcomes, thin proof.
  • Ad-hoc CMS blocks, slow page production, little reuse.
  • CRM noise: fields, stages, and follow-ups lacked discipline.

Approach

We ran an ICON sprint to lock positioning and narrative, build a reusable component library for the site, and install analytics + CRM hygiene—so pages ship faster, proof is front-and-center, and sales moves with clarity.

What we implemented

  • Identity: Positioning map, ICP use cases, expert voice, and a consistent visual kit.
  • Communication: Messaging playbook, offer ladders, and conversion-first page patterns.
  • Optimization: CMS component system, analytics events, and reporting cadence.
  • Navigation: KPI dashboard, monthly experiment plan, and closed-loop handoffs.

Solution

A unified story and systemized execution. We standardized voice and proof, created reusable CMS modules, and enforced CRM/analytics discipline to speed up production and tighten feedback loops.

  • Positioning & Narrative System: claims, objections, proof matrix.
  • Reusable Components: hero, stats, proof, offers, CTAs, resource tiles.
  • Sales Enablement: executive one-pager, deck shell, proposal template.
