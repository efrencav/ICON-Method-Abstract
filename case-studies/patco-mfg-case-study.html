Case Study — Industrial Manufacturing

Patco Manufacturing: Digital Upgrade for Faster Sales Cycles

Industry: Industrial Manufacturing, Fabrication, Engineering
Service Line: Website Wireframes, Messaging, Content Development, CMS Build, Light Identity Refresh

Overview

Patco operates in a competitive industrial manufacturing space where precision, speed, and reliability determine who wins the job. Their capabilities were strong, but their digital presence didn’t communicate the value or credibility behind their work. The site lacked structure, the RFQ flow created friction, and their messaging didn’t reflect the level of expertise buyers expect. The next step was clear. Build a modern website system that guides customers, strengthens trust, and supports faster quote requests.

The Challenge

Patco had the technical expertise and production capabilities, but their digital system held them back.

Primary issues we identified

  • A dated visual experience that weakened trust.
  • A website structure that didn’t guide buyers through categories or RFQ steps.
  • Messaging focused on features rather than outcomes and proof.
  • Inconsistent content tone and visuals.
  • No scalable CMS components, leading to slow content updates.
  • Logo usage that didn’t perform well in responsive layouts.

Approach

We executed a structured upgrade modeled after the Identity + Communication + Optimization tracks of the ICON Method™

What we implemented

  • Website Strategy & Wireframes: full wireframes for every major page/category with a logical hierarchy, clear content zones, and guided RFQ paths.
  • Messaging & Content: collaborative voice/tone, conversion-focused copy across categories, services, and RFQ flows, simplifying technical language.
  • Visual & Identity Enhancements: refined logo for digital clarity plus consistent spacing, typography, and usage rules.
  • CMS Development: modern CMS with reusable components and modular templates for categories, services, product details, and RFQ pages, built mobile-first with accessibility in mind.
  • Performance & Sales Support: analytics for RFQ/high-intent events, conversion-optimized templates, and a scalable content system the marketing team can own.

What We Delivered

A modern brand system and a faster content engine. We standardized voice/proof, built reusable page modules, and created sales-ready PDFs to support quoting conversations.

Identity & Visual System
  • Refined logo optimized for responsive layouts.
  • Updated typography and color usage for consistency.
  • Clean visual patterns aligned with industrial buyers.
Messaging Architecture
  • Clear value pillars tied to speed, precision, and reliability.
  • Category-level messaging that explains capabilities and use cases.
  • Strong CTAs and a guided RFQ path.
Website Wireframes & CMS Build
  • Full set of UX wireframes.
  • Modular CMS components for faster publishing.
  • SEO-ready structure and optimized page speed.
Content & Sales Enablement
  • Category pages, landing pages, and RFQ copy.
  • Streamlined messaging for quoting conversations.
  • Analytics and conversion events to measure performance.
